Skills
R


R is my programming language of choice. I first learned R in 2016, and it has turned out to be my favorite analysis tool. It's flexible, powerful, and straightforward (once you know what you're looking for).

Statistical Modeling


Statistical modeling helps us to create simplified representations of complex real-world phenomena. My favorite statistical modeling techniques are regression, multilevel modeling, factor analysis, and machine learning.

Data Visualization


To me, visualizations are one of the most powerful tools data analysts can use to convey meaning to a general audience. Images translate data into meaningful stories about the world.

Data Cleaning


I'm very detail-oriented. Before data is ready to analyze, I pay special attention to cleaning it, handling missing data and outliers, and checking that statistical assumptions have been met.

SPSS & Jamovi


I've also been trained in and taught students how to conduct statistical analyses in SPSS and Jamovi.

Communicating


I've been teaching courses on data analysis and statistics for seven years. One thing I have a passion for is figuring out how to discuss complex statistical concepts simply so they can be communicated to a general audience.
